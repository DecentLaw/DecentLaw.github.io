DecentLaw: A Center for Decentralized Law

This is a placeholder website for the DecentLaw project.

DecentLaw seeks to move legal document sourcing, maintenance and collaboration to git and GitHub. To allow lawyers to work with the tools of software coding. To improve the quality and speed of transacting, reduce sophistication barriers, to work at the speed of the web.

DecentLaw seeks to formalize the work of CommonAccord.org.

A pro-forma press-release version of the DecentLaw proposition.
